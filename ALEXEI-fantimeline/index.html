Drake

5 Interesting Facts

1.Drake is just his stage name, his full name is Aubrey Drake Graham.

2.His father is an African American from Memphis, Tennessee while his mom is a Jewish Canadian.

3.Drake starred on the show Degrassi: The Next Generation playing the character Jimmy Brooks who was disabled.

4.His hit songs include "Every Girl," "Best I Ever Had," "Money to Blow" and "Take Care."

5.Drake has done some voice acting as well. He appeared in Ice Age: Continental Drift in 2012, as Ethan, the young mammoth.
